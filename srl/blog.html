SRL 2026

Robust Cuffless Monitoring with Flexible Sensor Placement.

Wearable placement can make or break cuffless hemodynamic accuracy. Unlike other sensing modalities, our in silico study showed impedance plethysmography (#IPG) to be robust against user preference to wearable placement. \ We found no statistically significant differences with the #IPG sensors moved up to 10 mm with respect to the position of the radial artery. Reference: https://lnkd.in/g2GmkUrm

SRL 2026

Wearable Bioimpedance Smartwatch for Continuous Blood Pressure Monitoring

We introduce the first research-grade, fully wireless and wearable bioimpedance (BioZ) smartwatch capable of continuously monitoring cuffless blood pressure (BP) and blood velocity-related parameters. This work establishes a previously missing biophysical link between electrical bioimpedance signals and key hemodynamic metrics, allowing accurate, continuous monitoring without the need for a traditional cuff. The system was validated in the largest academic study of its kind, involving patients with hypertension (HTN) and cardiovascular disease (CVD). By translating theory into a real-world wearable device, this work represents a major step toward practical, continuous cardiovascular monitoring in everyday settings. Reference: https://lnkd.in/g2GmkUrm
