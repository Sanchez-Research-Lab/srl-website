About the Director

Benjamin Sanchez-Terrones, PhD

Benjamin Sanchez-Terrones is an Associate Professor at the University of Illinois Chicago, with joint appointments in Electrical and Computer Engineering and Biomedical Engineering. His research focuses on developing digital health technologies and wearable biosensors for non-invasive monitoring of physiological signals.

His work aims to improve how health is measured and understood through bioimpedance, machine learning, and physics-informed models. His lab develops wearable and clinical technologies for continuous monitoring, early disease detection, and more personalized healthcare solutions.
